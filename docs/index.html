FAQ

Workshops

What is the format of the classes?

Each class is four hours, with a water break in the middle.

Do I need to bring my own PC?

Yes, please bring your own laptop and make sure the that you have done the necessary installations.
Details of prerequisites will be provided in the event notification email as well as the event confirmation email.

Online Workshops

How long are the online classes?

Each online class is a 2 hour duration.

Do I need to install anything for the online workshops?

It depends on the class, Python 3.0 installation is required for the Python workshops.
Further details of requirements will be sent to you.
The classes are held over Google Hangout, so you will need to have a Google account to connect.

Do I need to code during the online workshops?

Yes, the online workshops are hands on workshops. And you will be required to code and follow along.

How do I know if I am successfully registered in the class?

You will be receive a confirmation email from me as soon as I have confirmed payment for your selected classes. Please include your transaction reference details on registration if your payment method does not match your registration name.

What if I couldn't attend a class/workshop?

You may decide to use your credits for a different class if you find that you are unable to attend the class you have registered for.
But please notify Aryn at arynchoong@yahoo.com at least 24 hours before the event.

Refunds are not allowed. Only one time transfer of class credits will be allowed.
